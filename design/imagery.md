# Fefe Ave — Imagery

Guidelines for photography and visuals so imagery supports the brand: warm, natural, and inviting.

---

## Overall style

Images should feel:

- **Bright** — Good light; avoid dark or murky shots.
- **Natural** — Real environments, natural light where possible.
- **Soft** — Gentle contrast; no harsh shadows or over-sharpening.
- **Warm** — Color temperature slightly warm; skin and fabrics look inviting.

---

## Preferred imagery

- **Clothing racks** — Product in context; boutique, organized.
- **Natural interiors** — Real spaces: shelves, windows, plants, soft decor.
- **Plants** — Potted plants, greenery; adds life and calm.
- **Textured fabrics** — Folds, knits, natural materials; tactile and premium.
- **Friendly candid photos** — Felicia and others as hosts: smiling, approachable, welcoming. Not stiff or corporate.

**Intention:** Felicia should feel like a **host welcoming people into her boutique** — present, warm, and real.

---

## Avoid

- **Dark photography** — Keeps the brand light and airy.
- **Harsh contrast** — No heavy shadows or over-processed HDR.
- **Overly saturated colors** — Stay within the warm, neutral palette; avoid neon or garish tones.
- **Generic stock feel** — Prefer authentic, on-brand shots over cold or anonymous imagery.

---

## Usage

- **Hero and key sections:** Strong, on-brand photos (Felicia, boutique, product in context).
- **Cards and features:** Supporting imagery that matches the same style (bright, natural, warm).
- **Admin dashboard:** Imagery is optional; when used (e.g. empty states, onboarding), keep it simple and consistent with the same style.

---

## Summary

Bright, natural, soft, warm. Prefer clothing racks, natural interiors, plants, textured fabrics, and friendly candid photos. Avoid dark, harsh, or oversaturated visuals. Felicia = host, not distant retailer.
