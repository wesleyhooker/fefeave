# Fefe Ave — Brand

Source of truth for brand personality, tone, and the difference between public and internal experiences.

---

## Brand personality

- **Approachable** — Felicia is the face of the brand; she should feel like a host, not a distant retailer.
- **Warm** — Inviting, not cold or corporate.
- **Elegant** — Refined without being stuffy.
- **Curated** — Thoughtful selection, not overwhelming inventory.
- **Trustworthy** — Reliable for resellers and buyers.

---

## Tone of voice

- **Inviting** — “Join Felicia,” “Catch the next drop,” “Discover.”
- **Editorial** — Confident, clear headlines; avoid jargon.
- **Community-driven** — Live shows, schedules, and shared excitement.
- **Human** — First person where it fits; avoid generic marketing speak.

---

## Brand feeling

The product should feel:

- **Premium but not intimidating** — Quality and care without exclusivity or coldness.
- **Minimal luxury** — Restraint and clarity over clutter.
- **Natural and light** — Airy, soft, warm (French boutique, natural light, soft fabrics).

---

## Public site vs admin dashboard

|                | Public marketing site                                          | Admin dashboard (internal)                           |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Purpose**    | Attract visitors, explain value, drive sign-ups and engagement | Run the business: data, workflows, accuracy          |
| **Tone**       | Warm, inviting, boutique, editorial, community-driven          | Clean, structured, data-focused                      |
| **Decoration** | Brand imagery, gold accents, serif headlines, personality      | Minimal decoration; clarity and hierarchy over flair |
| **Voice**      | Felicia-focused, “we” and “you,” friendly                      | Neutral, task-oriented, scannable                    |

**Rule:** Public site = brand-forward. Admin = function-first, with brand applied lightly (e.g. typography and color system only).
